ABOUT

Who are we?
We are a Toastmasters club.
You can call us an oratory hub.
Once every week,
We gather to speak
And afterwards go have some grub.

What are we like?
Students, staff, and alumni,
A monk, a priest and a rabbi.
You needn’t fear,
We’ve got 'em all here.
You’ll fit right in when you come by.

Interested? Come to our meetings or send us an email (info.varistytoastmasters@gmail.com). Join us on Facebook, where you can get up-to-date information about the club and chat with a current member.

What's the difference between Biztalk and Varsity?

Varsity is Auckland University's original Toastmasters club. We were so popular, Biztalk had to be created to ensure everyone had a chance to speak. Biztalk has its meetings on Monday and they take themselves very seriously

Why should I join this club?

Because we're fast and loose. If you're interested in listening to some squares talk about conflict resolution in the work place or whatever, look elsewhere. Sometimes we forget who was meant to be evaluating. Doesn't matter. Improvise. Adapt. Overcome.

What does 'Varsity' mean?

You uncultured swine! It refers to university.

I'm not a student. Can I still join?

Yes.

What's Toastmasters?

It's a bit like the illuminati. Only for public speaking. It's an international organisation that's over 100 years old (something like that. I can't be bothered looking it up) to help people get better at public speaking.
