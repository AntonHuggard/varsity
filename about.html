ABOUT US

Who are we?
We are a Toastmasters club.
You can call us an oratory hub.
Once every week,
We gather to speak
And afterwards grab some grub.

Where are we?
Our meetings occur in the Owen Glen
But don’t worry, you don’t need to flee!
We'll be waiting in Room 260-040c
So come along and have fun.

What are we like?
Students, staff, and alumni,
A monk, a priest and a rabbi,
You needn’t fear
We’ve got them all here
So you’ll fit right in when you come by

Interested? Come to our meetings or send us an email (info.varistytoastmasters@gmail.com). Check out the “Meetings” tab on our website to get further details on meeting times and location. Join us on Facebook, where you can get up-to-date information about the club and chat with a current member.
